OPEN ACCESS POLICY

All journals published by Research Sutra follow Open Access Model wherein no person or institution are charged for access to articles or other resources. Users are free to read, download, copy, distribute, print, search, or link to the full texts of papers published under a CC-BY-NC license and use these articles for any non-commercial purpose citing the link to the original publication without taking any permission.

Benefits of Open Access

Open Access Model of publication disseminates information widely and allows every person an unlimited and unrestricted access to knowledge and innovations thereby building better prospects for future research and greater impact. Research Sutra publishes all its journals under under a Creative Commons Attribution- NonCommercial 2.0 Generic (CC-BY-NC) License. Under this license, you need to give credit to the original creator. It allows reusers to distribute, remix, adapt, and build upon the material in any medium or format, for noncommercial purposes only. • . Authors publishing with Research Sutra journals retain copyright and they need not transfer copyright to the publisher. They retain all rights for their published work.
